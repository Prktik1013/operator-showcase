Brand identity guide

Harbor Lane Plumbing

Garden Grove CA - residential plumbing. Owner-answered line. Calm authority under pressure.

Logo

Minimum width 120px. Clear space = height of pipe cap. Do not stretch or recolor copper/navy ratio.

Color

Background cream

Ink navy

Accent copper

Typography

Fraunces - headlines

Source Sans 3 - body and UI

Voice

Short sentences. Local proof. No agency adjectives. Sounds like the owner on a job walk.

Don'ts

  • No purple gradients or Inter-default UI
  • No invented star counts or license numbers
  • No generic wrench stock icons
