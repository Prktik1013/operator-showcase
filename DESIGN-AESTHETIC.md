# DESIGN-AESTHETIC — Harbor Lane Plumbing

Bound to BRIEF taste dials for operator-showcase (Garden Grove residential plumber).

## Taste dials (BRIEF-bound)

DESIGN_VARIANCE: medium-high — staggered service cards, split-rail hero, copper accent rail on trust items; not a symmetric template grid.

MOTION_INTENSITY: moderate — hero fade-up sequence, hover lifts on CTAs; respects prefers-reduced-motion.

VISUAL_DENSITY: balanced — one hero photo, three trust signals, four services; no empty headline-only band or wall of badges.

## Palette

- Background: oklch(97% 0.008 95) cream field
- Surface: oklch(100% 0.002 95) card white
- Ink navy: oklch(24% 0.035 250) headings and footer
- Accent copper: oklch(58% 0.11 55) / #b87333 CTAs and trust rails

## Typography

- Display: Fraunces (warm serif, local-trades credibility)
- Body: Source Sans 3 (readable mobile copy)
- Scale: hero clamp, h2 clamp, 1.0625rem body per CREATIVE-CONTRACT.json

## Hero & imagery

Photo-led `assets/hero.webp` with navy scrim gradient — not stock-placeholder or text-only band. Logo mark in corner reinforces copper + navy identity.

## Layout composition

`split-rail-hero`: copy rail left, photo right (stacks on mobile). Section order: hero → trust → services → proof → about → area → cta.

## Motion budget

Moderate entrance animations on hero rail; transition hovers on phone CTA, primary button, service cards, and trust items. No glass/backdrop-filter tells.

## Anti-patterns to avoid

Generic house-triangle logos, 555 phones, agency filler copy, interchangeable competitor layout.
